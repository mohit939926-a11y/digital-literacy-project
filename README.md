## What is Digital Literacy?

Digital literacy means the ability to use digital devices, the internet, and technology safely and effectively.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Importance of Digital Literacy

• Helps students in online learning  
• Increases knowledge through the internet  
• Makes communication easier  
• Helps in career development  
• Important in modern education
